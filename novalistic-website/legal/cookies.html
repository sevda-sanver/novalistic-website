Cookie Policy

Last Updated: November 29, 2025

Novalistic.AI LLC ("we", "our", "us") uses cookies and similar technologies to improve website performance, analyze traffic, and provide a personalized user experience.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small files stored on your device that help websites function and track usage. They enable us to recognize your browser and capture certain information.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

A. Essential Cookies
Required for basic site operation and security. These cookies cannot be disabled as they are necessary for the website to function properly.

B. Analytics Cookies
Measure traffic and user behavior to help us understand how visitors interact with our website. Example: Google Analytics.

C. Marketing Cookies
Used for advertising optimization and tracking campaign effectiveness. Examples: Meta Pixel, LinkedIn Insight Tag.

3. Cookie Control

You may disable cookies through your browser settings. However, please note that some features may not work properly if cookies are disabled. Most web browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can modify your browser settings to decline cookies if you prefer.

4. Third-Party Cookies

Third parties (e.g., Google, Meta, LinkedIn) may set cookies when you visit our site. Their privacy practices apply to their cookies. We do not control these third-party cookies and recommend reviewing their privacy policies.

5. Cookie Duration

Session cookies are temporary and are deleted when you close your browser. Persistent cookies remain on your device for a set period or until you delete them manually.

6. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with a new "Last Updated" date.
